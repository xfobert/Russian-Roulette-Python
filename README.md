# Russian-Roulette-Python

RUSSIAN ROULETTE game in Python. Based in Orange Roulette's system.

There are three options in the game!

1: SHOOT YOURSELF

    As the name indicates, you put the gun to your head and pray the bullet is not in the chamber.

2: SPIN THE CYLINDER

    Spins the cylinder, thus randomizing the position of the bullet in the chamber.

3: SHOOT YOUR OPPONENT

    You shoot the other player, but there is a twist! If the other player survivesâ€¦ you will have to shoot yourself.

Every round, each player gets to choose the way they want to take their chances.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In order to run it, you just need to write this on your terminal once you are on it's repository:

           py russian_roulette.py

Once the game ends, you will have to rewrite it to play again.
